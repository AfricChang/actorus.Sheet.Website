## What formula support means here

AcTorus.Sheet is designed for workbook automation, so formula support focuses on the formula families users most often need in real `.xlsx` workflows.

## Common formula families

The product supports a useful range of formulas across areas such as:

- math
- text
- date and time
- lookup and reference
- logical formulas
- statistical formulas
- financial formulas

## Workbook calculation scenarios

The product is designed to handle scenarios such as:

- cross-sheet formulas
- named-range formulas
- workbook recalculation after data updates
- formula-driven output generation in backend services

## A practical expectation

The formula engine is intended for real automation workflows, but it is still important to review the limitations page if your files rely on less common spreadsheet features.
